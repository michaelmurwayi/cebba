About CEBBA

Coffee Estates' Bourgeoisie Brokers Agency Limited exists to fundamentally transform the coffee trading industry by addressing the challenges faced by coffee farmers and ensuring the delivery of high-quality coffee to the market...

Our clients range from individual farmers and large estates to coffee roasters, retailers, importers, and specialty coffee buyers. They also include cooperatives, NGOs, exporters, traders, and educational institutions...

Our Services

Direct Sale

Our direct sale services offer farmers and roasters a seamless connection, ensuring the best quality beans at fair prices. Our model guarantees transparency, sustainability, and unmatched flavor in every batch.

Brokerage

We specialize in connecting coffee producers with buyers to create a seamless, transparent, and fair marketplace ensuring the highest quality beans are sourced directly from trusted farmers and co-operatives.

Farmer Support

CEBBA is committed to empowering coffee farmers by providing support and fostering communication. Regular feedback and personalized assistance helps farmers improve their practices, achieve sustainable growth, and enhance the quality of their crops.

Quality Control

We are dedicated to delivering the finest coffee, with an unwavering commitment to quality at every stage. Our comprehensive quality control processes ensure that each batch of coffee beans consistently exceeds the highest industry standards.
