About Cebba

Coffee Estates' Bourgeoisie Brokers Agency Limited exists to fundamentally transform the coffee trading industry by addressing the challenges faced by coffee farmers and ensuring the delivery of high-quality coffee to the market. Our agency was established with a clear purpose and set of goals aimed at creating a sustainable, equitable, and farmer-friendly coffee trading environment.

Our clients range from individual farmers and large estates to coffee roasters, retailers, importers, and speciality Coffee buyers. They also include corperatives, NGOs, exporters, traders, and educational institutions. By catering to this diverse client base, the agency aims to create a holistic and integrated coffee trading ecosystem that benefits all participants and promotes sustainable and ethical practices throughout the supply chain. Our mission

Is to revolutionize the coffee brokerage industry by creating a sustainable, transparent, and equitable marketplace that prioritizes the well-being of coffee farmers. We are committed to providing exceptional value and support to our clients through innovative solutions, fair trade practices, and a deep respect for the art of coffee cultivation.

Our Vision

Is to be the leading coffee brokerage agency globally, renowned for our unwavering dedication to fostering farmer-friendly practices and ensuring the highest quality coffee reaches consumers. We aspire to bridge the gap between coffee growers and the market, empowering farmers to achieve prosperity and recognition for their hard work while delivering unparalleled service and excellence to our partners.
